Ask Us Directly
What sets ByteWave apart from other development agencies?
We combine Silicon Valley engineering practices with transparent communication and zero offshore risk. Our differentiators include fixed-price contracts with milestone-based payments, dedicated senior teams, 90%+ automated test coverage, and a money-back guarantee if we miss agreed deliverables. Our track record: 120+ projects, 98% on-time delivery, zero project failures.
What kinds of projects does ByteWave handle?
We're a full-stack software company serving clients in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and Europe. We specialise in custom web applications (React, Vue, Angular), mobile apps (Swift, Kotlin, React Native, Flutter), CRM/ERP systems, e-commerce platforms, SaaS products, LMS platforms, API development, and end-to-end digital transformation projects.
How much does a typical project cost?
Pricing depends on complexity and scope. Typical ranges: Simple website/landing page — $2,000–$5,000 (2–4 weeks). Custom web app — $10,000–$50,000 (6–12 weeks). Mobile app — $15,000–$75,000 (8–16 weeks). Enterprise CRM/ERP — $50,000–$200,000+ (12–24 weeks). We offer fixed-price, time & material, and dedicated team models. Schedule a free consultation for an exact quote.
How do you keep projects on track and communicate progress?
We follow Agile/Scrum with 2-week sprints, daily standups, and bi-weekly live demos. You get a dedicated project manager, access to our project board (Jira/Trello/Asana), and daily updates via Slack or Teams. We work in your time zone so you're never waiting for answers.
Do you provide post-launch maintenance and support?
Absolutely. We offer comprehensive 24/7 support packages covering security patches, performance monitoring, bug fixes with SLA-based response times, database optimisation, and new feature development. Every project includes 30 days of free post-launch support. Ongoing plans start from $400/month.
How do you ensure security and data protection?
We follow OWASP Top 10 security standards, conduct penetration testing and security audits on every major release, and ensure GDPR and SOC 2 compliance. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your IP is fully protected under strict NDAs signed before any project begins.
